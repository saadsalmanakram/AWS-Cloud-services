Amazon CloudFront (Content Delivery Network)

>> Best for:
   - Content-Heavy Websites: Websites delivering a large amount of static and dynamic content that needs to be distributed globally with low latency.

   - Media Streaming: Sites that stream video or audio content to users worldwide.

   - Security-Enhanced Sites: Websites that require DDoS protection and HTTPS for content delivery.

>> Examples: Streaming platforms, large-scale blogs, global e-commerce sites, media-heavy websites, web applications needing fast content delivery across the globe.
